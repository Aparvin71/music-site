What’s New in v40.7.0

A running list of key changes included in the landing-page layout refresh and continued modular cleanup.

v40.7.0

Landing Page Card Refresh

  • Kept the music library as the landing experience.
  • Kept the About This Music card on the music page.
  • Moved the support cards into a cleaner top-of-page order.
  • Placed Get the App first, followed by Mission, Fill Your Mind, and Explore More Pages.
  • Bumped app and cache versioning for a cleaner rollout.

Get the App first

The install card now appears first in the top summary area so the app-install option is easier to find right away.

Homepage card order

The homepage now flows from music hero into Get the App, then Mission, then Fill Your Mind, then Explore More Pages, while keeping the About card on the music page.

Music stays front and center

The landing experience remains the music library, so visitors still arrive directly into the listening and discovery experience.

Prior modular work retained

The earlier modular cleanup remains in place for lyrics, media session, filters, queue handling, featured collection UI, playlists, player sheet behavior, offline flow, and album pages.
