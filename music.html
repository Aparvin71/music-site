About This Music

Every song here begins with words I have written. I use AI as a creative tool to help shape the music, sound, and vocals, allowing those lyrics to become fully realized songs.

My hope is to share music that is heartfelt, thought-provoking, and encouraging. Many songs draw from Scripture and the Christian faith, pointing toward worship, reflection, hope, and truth.
