Aineo v43.1.61 battery optimization pass

Changes made:
- Visualizer only runs when full player is open, track is playing, page is visible, and low-power mode is off.
- Visualizer draw loop throttled to ~15 fps in low-power mode and ~30 fps otherwise.
- Background playback warmup reduced in low-power mode.
- Audio/analysis prefetch scaled back in low-power mode.
- Visibility change now stops visualizer and lyrics sync while hidden, and resumes when visible.
- Low-power mode auto-enables on reduced-motion, save-data, and smaller-screen devices.
- Starfield animation is disabled in low-power mode to cut GPU usage.

Package remains R2-only with no bundled audio files.
