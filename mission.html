Mission

The Great Commission

Aineo Music exists to use songs, lyrics, and creative work to point people to Jesus Christ, strengthen believers in truth, and support the call to go, make disciples, teach God’s word, and proclaim the gospel.

This site is meant to be more than a place to stream music. It is meant to be a place that helps direct the heart toward worship, reflection, conviction, hope, and obedience to Christ.

Matthew 28:19–20 Mark 16:15 Romans 10:14–15 2 Timothy 4:2

What This Means

Music With Purpose

The goal is not simply to make songs, but to make songs that carry truth, stir worship, encourage repentance, remind people of scripture, and help keep the mind fixed on what honors God.

Some songs are directly rooted in biblical themes. Others reflect daily life, family, struggle, gratitude, joy, or personal moments. In all of it, the deeper desire is that what is created here would be useful, honest, and pointed in a better direction.

Mind & Heart

Fill Your Mind With What Is Good

What goes in eventually comes out.

What we repeatedly take in through music, words, images, entertainment, and attention will shape our thoughts, desires, attitudes, and speech. Trash in, trash out. But the opposite is also true: truth in, truth out; worship in, worship out; scripture in, scripture out.

  • Guard the input. Be careful what you allow to settle into your thoughts and affections.
  • Choose what feeds life. Make room for truth, beauty, worship, wisdom, and what is pure.
  • Watch the fruit. What comes out often reveals what has been stored within.
  • Practice renewal. Let God reshape the mind and heart through His word.
Philippians 4:8 Luke 6:45 Proverbs 4:23 Romans 12:2 Colossians 3:1–2
