Suggested Upload Checklist

  1. Upload the MP3 to your audio folder or R2 bucket.
  2. Upload the cover image to your covers folder.
  3. Upload the matching .lrc file into the lyrics/ folder.
  4. Append the generated JSON snippet to tracks.json.
  5. Refresh the site and verify the song appears with lyrics and scripture links.
